Midnight sun

Do you know the phenomenon?



What is the midnight sun?

The sun appearing at midnight. In the middle of the night when the sky is normally a velvety blanket of darkness, the sun can clearly still be seen.

Duration

Around the summer solstice (approximately 21 June in the Northern Hemisphere and 23 December in the Southern Hemisphere)
